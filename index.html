FAQ

Can I edit translations?

Yes—every locale can be overridden before publishing.

Do you store my Apple keys?

Keys are encrypted at rest; you can revoke them at any time in App Store Connect.

Does it change my app binary or metadata?

Only the localized ‘What’s New’ and Promotional Text fields for the selected version.

Which locales are supported?

All locales supported by App Store Connect; you choose which to publish.

Can I use my own glossary?

Yes—add terms to keep brand names and feature names consistent.
